Credits

Some of the tools, code and images used in this game was sourced from the internet. These sources are listed below.

The explosions were created by me (Martin Conway) using Positech's explosion generator and converted to the correct format using Adobe Photoshop. The remaining graphics were designed from scratch by me in Adobe Illustrator.
