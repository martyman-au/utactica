UTACTICA

In the distant future two competing tribes battle for control of the newly settled world of Utactica. The Mecritians and the Revekians blood feud pre-dates any existing histories. Each newly discovered world is a battle ground, its resources and the honour of winning up for grabs.

Take control of one of these tribes and use Utactica's resources to overpower your opponent, destroy their outpost and claim this world for your own.

Food resourcs allow you to expand your expiditionary force, while science resources can be used to research new technologies to give you the upper hand.
